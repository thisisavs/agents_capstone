### **Competitor News Digest: Mjolnir Power Systems**
**A summary of recent strategic maneuvers in the data center power market by Eaton, Schneider Electric, and Vertiv.**

---

### **Eaton News**

1.  **Eaton to Acquire Boyd Thermal for $9.5 Billion, Venturing into Liquid Cooling**:
    (2025-11-03) Eaton has signed an agreement to acquire the Boyd Thermal business of Boyd Corporation, a move that will significantly expand its data center cooling solutions to include advanced liquid cooling technology. This acquisition positions Eaton to provide comprehensive power and cooling solutions for high-density computing and AI data center applications.
    Source: [Eaton Signs Agreement to Acquire Boyd Thermal, Expanding Solutions for Data Center Customers to Include Critical Liquid Cooling Technology](https://www.eaton.com/us/en-us/company/news-insights/news-releases/2025/eaton-signs-agreement-to-acquire-boyd-thermal--expanding-solutio.html)

### **Schneider Electric News**

1.  **Schneider Electric Secures $2.3 Billion in U.S. Data Center Deals**:
    (2025-11-19) Schneider Electric has announced two major partnerships with U.S. data center operators, Switch and Digital Realty, valued at a combined total of nearly $2.3 billion. The agreements will see Schneider Electric provide power modules, cooling systems, uninterruptible power supplies (UPS), and switchgear to support the growing infrastructure demands of AI.
    Source: [Schneider Electric seals $2.3 billion in US data centre deals to power AI boom](https://www.reuters.com/technology/schneider-electric-seals-23-billion-us-data-centre-deals-power-ai-boom-2025-11-19/)
2.  **Schneider Electric Launches EcoStruxure Foresight Operation**:
    (2025-11-29) Schneider Electric has introduced EcoStruxure Foresight Operation, a new AI-powered platform that integrates energy, power, and building management systems into a single interface. This solution is designed to provide data center operators with enhanced operational intelligence and efficiency.
    Source: [Schneider Electric Introduces EcoStruxure Foresight Operation](https://www.achrnews.com/articles/154129-schneider-electric-introduces-ecostruxure-foresight-operation)
3.  **New White Paper on Powering AI Training Data Centers**:
    (2025-11-18) Schneider Electric has published a new data bulletin detailing the unique power system requirements for AI training data centers. The document provides insights into the infrastructure needed to support the intensive computing resources for training AI language models.
    Source: [Power System Needs for AI Training Data Centers](https://www.se.com/ww/en/download/document/WP_Power_System_Needs_for_AI_Training_Data_Centers/)

### **Vertiv News**

1.  **Vertiv and Caterpillar to Co-develop Power Solutions for Data Centers**:
    (2025-11-18) Vertiv has entered into a strategic collaboration with Caterpillar to develop integrated energy solutions for data centers. The partnership will focus on creating pre-designed and validated systems to improve efficiency, and resiliency and to accelerate deployment timelines for data center power infrastructure.
    Source: [Vertiv and Caterpillar Announce Energy Optimization Collaboration to Expand End-to-End Power and Cooling Offerings for AI Data Centers](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/vertiv-and-caterpillar-announce-energy-optimization-collaboration-to-expand-end-to-end-power-and-cooling-offerings-for-ai-data-centers-302298136.html)
2.  **Vertiv Partners with IIT Bombay for Advanced Cooling Research**:
    (2025-11-28) Vertiv has announced a collaboration with the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay to research and develop advanced cooling solutions for AI-powered data centers. This partnership aims to address the thermal management challenges posed by high-density computing workloads.
    Source: [Vertiv and IIT Bombay join hands to advance cooling strategies for AI powered data centres](https://www.crn.in/news/vertiv-and-iit-bombay-join-hands-to-advance-cooling-strategies-for-ai-powered-data-centres/)
